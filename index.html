To Malad

From the moment you became part of my world, everything became softer, sweeter, and somehow more real.
You are the calm in my chaos, the warmth in my silence, and the dream I never want to wake up from.
No matter how many days pass, I’ll keep choosing you — in every version of time, in every possible world. You’re not just special, you’re everything.
Still falling for you — Tayssir
